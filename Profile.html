Profile:

  • Chuancang Ding is currently an Associate Professor and Master’s Supervisor with Soochow University (SUDA), Suzhou, China. He received the Ph.D. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Xi’an Jiaotong University, Xi’an, China, in 2021, and the B.Eng. degree in Mechanical Design, Manufacturing and Automation from Shandong University, Jinan, China, in 2016.

    He has long been engaged in research on pattern recognition, machine vision, artificial intelligence, and signal processing, with particular focus on deep learning, explainable artificial intelligence, multimodal visual perception, non-stationary signal processing, and weak feature extraction. His research serves practical needs in high-end equipment and complex industrial systems, including condition perception, defect identification, fault diagnosis, and health assessment.

    He has presided over a number of competitive research projects, including one General Program and one Young Scientists Fund project of the National Natural Science Foundation of China, one Young Scientists Fund project of the Jiangsu Natural Science Foundation, two China Postdoctoral Science Foundation projects, and one open project of a State Key Laboratory, as well as railway-related open cooperation projects and multiple industry-sponsored projects.

    As the first author or corresponding author, he has published more than 30 SCI/EI papers in reputable journals such as IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics, IEEE Transactions on Industrial Electronics, Reliability Engineering & System Safety, Advanced Engineering Informatics, Knowledge-Based Systems, and related journals. His papers include multiple CAS Q1 and Q2 publications, with several ESI Highly Cited Papers. His work has received more than 1,800 citations on Google Scholar. He has also applied for or been granted more than 20 invention patents.

    He was selected for the World’s Top 2% Scientists List in 2024 and 2025. He currently serves as Deputy Director of the SUDA–Shanghai Yuanpeng Collaborative Innovation Center for Traction System Condition Monitoring, a Youth Committee Member of the Intelligent Operation and Maintenance Branch of the Chinese Mechanical Engineering Society, a Youth Working Committee Member of the Jiangsu Society for Vibration Engineering, and a Guest Editor for a special issue of Electronics.

    In teaching, he has guided students to win more than 10 national and provincial competition awards in contests such as the Global Campus AI Algorithm Elite Competition, China Robot Competition, and the Lanqiao Cup. He teaches undergraduate and graduate courses including Artificial Intelligence, Mechanical Drawing, and Intelligent Operation and Maintenance of Transportation Equipment.

Research Interests:

  • Explainable Intelligent Fault Diagnosis and Condition Monitoring:
    Developing interpretable intelligent diagnosis methods for rotating machinery and rail transit equipment under complex operating conditions, with emphasis on sparse optimization, transfer learning, continual learning, and trustworthy AI.
  • Machine Vision and Defect Identification:
    Investigating image understanding, defect segmentation, and visual inspection methods for industrial surfaces and equipment, especially robust visual perception under noisy, variable, and real-world environments.
  • Multimodal Perception and Data Fusion:
    Studying multimodal and multi-source information fusion for industrial condition awareness, integrating signals, images, and operational data to improve perception reliability and diagnostic accuracy.
  • Non-stationary Signal Processing and Weak Feature Extraction:
    Exploring advanced time-frequency analysis, adaptive decomposition, sparse representation, and weak fault feature enhancement methods for non-stationary and strong-interference scenarios.
  • Health Assessment for High-end Equipment and Complex Industrial Systems:
    Focusing on bearing systems, gearboxes, RV reducers, axle-box bearings, and railway traction-related equipment, aiming at fault diagnosis, degradation assessment, and health management in industrial applications.
