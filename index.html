Chuancang Ding, a Professor with the School of Control Science and Engineering, Shandong University (SDU), Jinan, China. Dr. Cong is the director of Multimedia and Vision Processing Group (MVP Group), which is affiliated to the Key Laboratory of Machine Intelligence & System Control, Ministry of Education. His current research interests include Artificial Intelligence, Pattern Recognition, Machine Learning, Low-altitude Intelligence, and Embodied Perception. He has published more than 120 IEEE/ACM Trans/CCF-A papers, and has received more than 20 awards, including the IEEE SPS Best Paper Award and IEEE Chester W. Sall Award.
