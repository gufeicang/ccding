Chuancang Ding is an Associate Professor and Master's Supervisor at Soochow University. His research focuses on artificial intelligence, pattern recognition, machine vision, and signal processing, with particular interests in deep learning, explainable AI, multimodal visual perception, non-stationary signal processing, and weak feature extraction. He has led several competitive research projects, including NSFC programs and China Postdoctoral Science Foundation projects, and has published more than 30 SCI/EI-indexed papers in journals such as IEEE TII, IEEE TIE, RESS, AEI, and KBS. His work has received over 1,800 citations on Google Scholar, and he was selected for the World's Top 2% Scientists List (Annual Impact) in 2024 and 2025.
